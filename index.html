Our Story

A Legacy of Flavour in the Heart of Osu

White House Restaurant has been serving authentic Ghanaian cuisine to the vibrant community of Osu, Accra for years. Founded on the belief that good food brings people together, we pride ourselves on using fresh, locally sourced ingredients to craft dishes that honour our rich culinary heritage.

From the smoky aroma of our peppered goat head to the refreshing sweetness of fresh seafood, every plate tells a story of Ghana's diverse food culture.
